Personal

My name is Lucas DiCioccio. I prefer to define myself as a scientist/engineer generalist. However I lean and find creative ways to apply advanced techniques to software engineering. I am also a learning-dad and an hobbyist photographer.

Experience

I’ve been around quite some time and I have seen and taken a variety of roles. I’ve gone through a PhD programme, enjoyed it a lot but really preferred the materialistic benefits of higher-paying jobs than what French Academia has to offer. I’ve worked on small and extremely-large systems, microteams to mega-corp. I’ve done research, development, and operational work and found an interest in each of these fields.
